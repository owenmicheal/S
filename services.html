Kola Loan

This is Our flagship product that has over 80% of our entire Loan portfolio. It's the most popular product because under this product we are able to provide loans to our customer with loan repayments made on a daily basis which is convienient ti our customers.

The loan repayment period is three months which is convienient to the low income earners to repay the loan.

Season Loan

This is a product that focuses on the different money making seasons in the various sectors were our clients belong. If we are to take the Kalerwe business community, it has aboout three major seaasons, back to school season whwere the major productsare school materials that range from books to soft drinks that students take with them to school.

In short, the Loan is intended to boast our client's Businesses for this short period and should be paid with in the period of that particular season, Repayments of these Loans are done daily a well.

School Fees Loan

This is an edducation credit program that we offer. This type of LOan is strictly for school fees and not businesses. The Loan applicant is asked to present the bank slip or any documentation from the institution to help in the verification of the true purpose of the Loan. The Loan runs the same period as the school term or semester not more.

This interest rate fot this particular loan is lower given that it's not being invested in an instant profit making project. The repayments still are done daily as othe loans.

Gagawala Loan

Tis is an asset finance LOan, with this type of Loan, the applicant should have had an item in mind that he or she needs to finance.

He/she should have notified Sanyulyo six months or more of this intention the client should have deposited a half of the total cost of the asset in question on his loan Account of Gagawala, Sanyulyo then tops up the other half and all the purchase agreements are kept in safe custody withSanyulyo untill the credit obligations are fully met. When the client clears the loan, all the asset documents are handed over and the client is free to apply for another Loan if he/she wishes.
