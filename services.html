Kola Loan

This is a business Loan product that is meant to financially aid the day today business activities of our clients.

This Loan product is payable in three months at an affordable interest rate.

School Fees Loan

This Loan product enables a parent to access funds to clear school fees and it is payable in affordable installments for a period of three (3) months.

This Loan is paid to the third party.

Season Loan

This loan product is designed to help customers with seasonal businesses, to meet cashflow needs throughout their business cycle.

This loan is payable in a period of three months with daily installments.
